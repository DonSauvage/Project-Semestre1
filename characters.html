Main Page   Character Creation  

Here you will be able to created your own character.

How it works :

Once you click on "Alright Mate" : The program will create 4 random numbers 6 times and add them into an array.
Then it will calculate the sum of each array.
Each array will now represent each one of your stats (str, dex, int, etc)
After that, 2 bonuses will be added, depending on the class and race you have chosen.
If for example you have chosen a Dwarf Fighter your bonuses will be : str +2, dex -2, con +3, int +0, wis +2, char +0











The race, class and gender you choose affect your stats and the age generator.
(These are only the basic races from the player's handbook)
